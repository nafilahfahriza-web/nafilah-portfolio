TokoBerkah — E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Data Analysis · Excel · Dummy Project

Overview

Analysed 800 orders, 200 customers, and 24 products across January–December 2025 for a dummy Indonesian e-commerce store. The goal was to answer four business questions around monthly trends, product profitability, and regional revenue.

Key Insights

  • 1December recorded 17× more orders than January (156 vs 9), confirming strong year-end seasonal demand driven by Harbolnas and holiday shopping.
  • 2The best-selling product by volume (Lampu LED Hias 5m, 537 units) was also the most profitable — a rare double-win that makes it the store's strategic hero product.
  • 3Rice Cooker Mini ranked #1 in revenue but only #3 in profit, showing that high-priced items don't always yield the best margins.
  • 4Jakarta led in both order count (170) and revenue (Rp57 M), while Denpasar outranked Bandung in revenue despite fewer orders — indicating higher average order values per customer.

Tools & Skills

Microsoft Excel PivotTable & PivotChart VLOOKUP (multi-table) Data Cleaning Revenue & Profit Calculation Seasonal Trend Analysis

Dataset

800 orders · 200 customers · 24 products · 4 tables (orders, order_items, products, customers) · Jan–Dec 2025

Warung Selera Nusantara — F&B Restaurant Analysis

Data Analysis · Excel · Dummy Project

Overview

Analysed 850 transactions across 4 restaurant branches in Jakarta, Bandung, Depok, and Surabaya for January–December 2025. Focus areas: operational scheduling, menu strategy, and branch performance benchmarking.

Key Insights

  • 1Two daily peak hours emerged: 12:00 (117 transactions) and 19:00 (107 transactions) — a classic F&B double-peak pattern useful for staff scheduling.
  • 2Weekend days (Sunday 166, Saturday 163) generated nearly twice the weekday average, signalling a family/leisure dining pattern rather than a weekday office crowd.
  • 3Ayam Geprek Sambal Bawang dominated all three metrics — volume, revenue, and profit — with 694 units sold, nearly 3× its closest competitor. A true hero product.
  • 4Sudirman (Jakarta) contributed ~36% of all transactions, almost double the lowest-performing branch (Dago, Bandung), suggesting location-driven demand differences worth investigating.

Tools & Skills

Microsoft Excel PivotTable (Group by Hours) TEXT() for day extraction VLOOKUP (3-table join) Data Cleaning (PROPER) Branch Comparison

Dataset

850 transactions · 4 branches · 24 menu items · 4 tables (transaksi, transaksi_detail, menu, cabang) · Jan–Dec 2025

AdaPromo Digital — Marketing Campaign Analysis

Data Analysis · Excel · Dummy Project

Overview

Evaluated 15 digital ad campaigns across Google Ads, Meta Ads, and TikTok Ads throughout 2025. Calculated performance metrics (CTR, CVR, CPA, ROAS) from raw daily impression and spend data to identify which platforms and campaigns delivered the best return on investment.

Key Insights

  • 1Google Ads delivered the highest ROAS overall, led by the "Search Brand Defense" campaign — high purchase intent from branded search queries drove strong conversion rates.
  • 2"Story Ads Eksperimen" (Meta Ads) had the lowest ROAS despite a sizeable budget, identifying it as the clearest candidate for budget reallocation.
  • 3"Konten Viral Challenge" (TikTok Ads) showed a high CTR but low CVR — a classic funnel drop-off where users clicked but did not convert, suggesting a landing page or offer mismatch.
  • 4Harbolnas campaigns (11.11 & 12.12) performed above their respective platform averages, confirming that seasonal sale moments amplify campaign effectiveness across all channels.

Tools & Skills

Microsoft Excel VLOOKUP CTR / CVR / CPA / ROAS Calculation PivotTable Funnel Analysis Data Cleaning & Validation

Dataset

15 campaigns · 3 platforms · 958 daily performance rows · 2 tables (campaign, performance_harian) · Jan–Dec 2025
